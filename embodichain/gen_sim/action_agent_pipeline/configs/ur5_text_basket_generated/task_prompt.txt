two apple objects into the wicker_basket.

the left UR5 grasps the left apple while the right UR5 grasps the right
apple in the same nominal graph edge. After both apples are
grasped, the placement phase must be sequential: the left UR5 places its
apple into the wicker_basket and returns to its initial
pose, then the right UR5 places its apple into the
wicker_basket and returns to its initial pose.

- left_arm must only manipulate `left_apple`.
- right_arm must only manipulate `right_apple`.
- Both target objects must be released into `wicker_basket`.

Generate one deterministic nominal graph with the following semantic sequence.
Do not add extra alignment, search, recovery, or monitor steps. Do include the
specified post-release retreat and return-to-initial steps so the arm that just

wicker_basket. For each arm, the placement order must be: move
to a high staging pose above the container, lower to the release pose inside the
